PRS: A LOOK IN THE MUSIC INDUSTRY

Things you should know

A typical PRS instrument is built to extremely high quality standards, looks exquisite, is comfortable to use, and highly versatile, capable of a wide range of sounds. All this luxury implies quite high sales prices, which make PRS guitars for professional musicians or amateurs who take their hobby very seriously..

Finally, PRS offers a full line of boutique guitar amplifiers. These amplifiers range from vintage designs from the golden era of classic rock to high-gain amps ideal for modern rock. They are highly versatile, and custom models are available with beautiful wood finishes.

Exotic hardwoods and quality American craftsmanship come at a price, but those are the foundations on which the company's success has been built.
